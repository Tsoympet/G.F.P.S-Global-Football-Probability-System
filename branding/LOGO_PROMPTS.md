# GFPS – Logo Prompts & Branding Notes

## 1. Primary Logo Prompt (for AI image tools)

> “Minimalist, professional football analytics logo for a brand called ‘G.F.P.S – Global Football Probability System’.  
> Dark background, electric blue and emerald green accents.  
> A stylized football combined with a clean probability curve or graph lines.  
> Futuristic but sober, fintech / data-science aesthetic.  
> Flat vector style, high contrast, suitable as an app icon and website logo.”

---

## 2. Alternative Variations

### A. Icon-Only (App Icon)

> “Square app icon, black background, simple and bold letters ‘GFPS’ with modern geometric font.  
> Subtle glowing line chart or probability curve behind the letters.  
> Football motif integrated into the letter ‘O’ shape or curve.  
> Minimal, easily recognizable at small sizes.”

### B. Badge with Shield

> “Football analytics crest logo: shield shape with a stylized football at the bottom and a probability curve at the top.  
> Text ‘GFPS’ centered.  
> Colors: deep black, electric blue, emerald green, white.  
> Modern, esports + data-science hybrid style.”

### C. Typography-First

> “Wordmark logo ‘GFPS’ with the subtitle ‘Global Football Probability System’ below.  
> Thin, precise sans-serif font, slightly futuristic.  
> Small abstract icon of a ball + chart on the left.  
> Designed for website header and documentation.”

---

## 3. Color Palette

- **Electric Blue** – `#007BFF`  
- **Emerald Green** – `#00C46B`  
- **Carbon Black** – `#101010`  
- **Pure White** – `#FFFFFF`

Use dark backgrounds plus blue/green highlights for a “data / analytics / AI” mood.

---

## 4. Font Suggestions

- Titles / logo:
  - Montserrat, Poppins, Inter, or similar clean geometric sans-serifs.
- Body text:
  - Inter / Roboto / System font.

---

## 5. Usage Notes

- The logo must look good:
  - as app icon
  - as favicon
  - in dark UI theme
  - on black or very dark grey background
- Avoid:
  - cartoonish styles
  - gradients that don’t scale well
  - overly complex shapes.
